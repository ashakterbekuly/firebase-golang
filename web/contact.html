About ArchNEO

ArchNEO is an architectural web-site that connects architects, designers, and architecture enthusiasts from all around the world. Our platform provides a space where you can join design meetups, view portfolios of architects, and more - all available online and on our mobile app.

Solution to a big problem

Many companies and organizations were in need of experienced professionals in the field of architecture. The same professionals needed help finding new clients and jobs. To solve these problems, ArchNeo was born.

Our Mission

Help customers and specialists of architecture field find each other to solve pressing problems.

Our Vision

ArchNeo is the most familiar and reliable way to search for architecture specialists according to your criteria. ArchNeo specialists are a community of progressive professionals capable of responding to the needs of each client and employer.
